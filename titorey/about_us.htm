Message from the Vice President:

 

Mabuhay! Welcome to Tito Rey of the Islands! Home of the Filipino Stars.

 

Tito Rey of the Islands is one of the pioneer Filipino American restaurants in the San Francisco Bay Area. It has served the FilAm community since the 1980’s. From

venue preparation to food catering and live entertainment, the new Tito Rey of the Islands has so much to offer now more than ever. With its fine dining and excellent service, Tito Rey has won the hearts of its customers. As you may already know, Tito Rey has become a favorite haven for Filipino celebrities as well as prominent personalities here and from the Philippines. Recently, it has been the venue for Philippine senators like Hon. Franklin Drillon, Hon. Raul Rocco, Hon. Nikki Coseteng, and Hon. Edgardo Angara. Not to mention showbiz personalities like: Martin Nieverra, Gary Valenciano, Regine Velasquez, Lani Misalucha among many others.

 

Known for its cozy atmosphere, fine dining, excellent music, and state-of-the-art discotheque, Tito Rey has also become a Filipino American landmark in Daly City. Through the years, it has paved the way to serving our countrymen with the best customer service competitive in the mainstream market.

Our spacious 200-people capacity dining hall gives our banquet guests a memorable experience. The karaoke bar adjacent to the hall has a room for 100 people, enough to create an intimate impression. We also serve a wide array of Japanese sushi just next to the bar.

 

Come to Tito Rey and experience it yourself. Our friendly staffs are more than happy to accommodate your next gathering. We are open Tuesdays-Saturdays, from 5pm to 2am; Sundays and Mondays are open for banquet and private parties.

Thank you and we look forward to serving you soon.

Danny Tamondong
