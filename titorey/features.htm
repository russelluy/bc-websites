Banquet General Information & Operating Procedure 

 

Hours of Operations: The Tito Rey Restaurant is available for Sundays through Saturdays. The time frames are 9:00 am to 2:30 p.m. Or 6:00 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. any addition hours before 11:00 am or after closing 10:00 p.m. will incur a $200.00 surcharge (per hour) added labor. The Tito Rey Restaurant normally closed on Monday. The restaurant can be open. But, there is a surcharge of $500.00 added to the cost of the food and beverage. Monday affairs may start at anytime, but there is a maximum of 5 hours usage time.

       

  • Ball room (Max 100/150): $ 150.00

  • Kapihan: (Max40/60): $ 50.00

  • Cocktail Lounge: (Max 40/50): $50.00

Deposit: A Non-refundable deposit of $200.00 is required to hold a reservation.  We will hold reservations for seven days without a deposit. After the seven days, if no deposit is received the reservation is canceled. If the reservation is canceled or changed the deposit is retained. Requests for cancellations of affairs must be submitted in writing. Tito Rey Restaurant  requires a meeting 30 days prior to the affairs so the participants can write a contract. This insures our ability to supply all of the catering needs to your affair. At this time a second deposit of 50% of the estimated cost is due. If a change of reservation is made then the first deposit is retained. If a cancellation or change is made after the second deposit and the room is not re-booked, one half of the deposit will be retained by Tito Rey Restaurant to assist with the loss of revenue. No decorations or objects may be attached in anyway to walls or the permanent structure of the restaurant. The sponsor will be liable for any damages. Any rice, birdseed, rose petals, confetti, ETC., thrown in or around the restaurant will incur a $250.00 clean up fee.

 

Food and Beverages: The Tito Rey Restaurant must supply all food items. Menu selections, room requirements and all other arrangements must be received thirty days prior to the function. These menus are only a general suggestion of the variety of entrees and complements available. We take great pride in developing specific ideas and themes or simply expressing your taste in a personally tailored menu. No food may be taken off Tito Rey Restaurant. If alcoholic beverages are to be served on the restaurant premises (or elsewhere under the restaurant’s beverage license.) Tito Rey Restaurant will require that beverage be provided and dispensed only by the restaurant servers and bartenders. The restaurant’s alcoholic beverage license requires the restaurant to (1) request proper identification (photo ID) of any person of questionable age and refuse alcoholic beverage service if the person is either under age or proper identification cannot be produced and (2) refuse alcoholic beverage service to any person who, in the Manager on duty’s judgment, appears intoxicated. All food and beverage prices are subject to change or if unable to attain specified order, substitutions may take place.

 

Beverage Charges: For parties of 25 or more. A bar will be set up in your room. Charges for a Host or cash Bar are $ 50.00 for first hour, $25.00 for each additional hour. If you do not wish a bar, liquor service will not be available to your group.

 

Photocopies and Fax: The Catering Department will be happy to make photocopies for your group. A charge of 15 cents each will be applied to your bill. Fax services are available at $5.00 per page.

 

Service Charge and Tax: A service charge of 18% will be added to all food and beverage. California law states that service charge is subject to sales tax. If your organization is exempt from California State Tax, the proper exemption form must be submitted to Tito Rey Restaurant when contracts are signed.

 

Cakes: Cakes from commercial bakeries may be brought in to restaurant; delivery will be accepted one hour prior to the scheduled arrival of your event. A service charge of $2.00 per person is added on to all such cakes. If you wish Tito Rey Restaurant supply your cake, we will need the following information: type of cake, filling and frosting, inscription and design.

 

Audio/Visual: VCR and screen is available in ballroom and Cocktail Lounge only. $75.00 per session Podium or microphone is available for a fee of $35.00. DJ is available for $150.00 for the first hour. The additional hours are 50.00 per hour.

 

Skirting: Skirting is available for tables and special effect for you affair. Charges are listed below:

                             Head table: $20.00                Gift/ Registration Table $ 10.00

                             Cake table:  $20.00                           Buffet Table:    $ 20.00

                             Beverage table: $10.00

 

Parking: Parking is available on location. Tito Rey Restaurant is not responsible for losses or damage to vehicle and its contents.

         

Security: If, in the sole judgment of Tito Rey Restaurant. Security is required in order to maintain order due to and nature of your event, Tito Rey Restaurant may require you to provide, at your expense, uniformed or non-uniformed security personnel.
