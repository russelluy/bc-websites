Hardwood Flooring - Done Right.
One satisfied customer at a time

Hi Tech Hardwood Floors was established in 1994 with the goal of providing our customers with the very best in hardwood flooring craftsmanship and customer service experience you should expect from quality contractor. Owner Andy Tran has personally installed, sanded & refinished hardwood floors in literality thousands of homes over the past 13 years.

Along with his trained team of technicians who are experts in every aspect of installing, sanding, staining, and finishing your hardwood floors. You can rest assured that it will get done right the first time. From your initial free estimate, to job scheduling and finishing your project on time. You’ll enjoy peace of mind along every step of the way.

Don’t be surprised if Owner Andy Tran is personally greeting you on your next job, quality work is what he’s all about.
