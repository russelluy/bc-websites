Answers to common questions

Carpet Facts

Q)  What flooring products can I choose from?
A)  We install hardwood, laminate, carpet, tile, and
vinyl from top-quality brands like Mirage
Hardwoods, BHK, Alloc, Duraceramic, and
Mohawk/Royalty carpets.

Q)  Do you offer traditional hardwood services?
A)  Yes, we experts in hardwood, and perform the
full spectrum of traditional wood services, like
sanding, staining, refinishing and recoating.

Q)  Do you provide any upfront design consultation?
A)  Yes, we are pleased to offer complimentary
consultation. Our approach gives you personal
assistance in helping you match the perfect floor to
your home and your life.

Preparing for Installation

Q)  Do I need to remove our furniture?
A)  We offer full service, and will move all your furniture
from your home to start our work. When the floor is
complete, we’ll return furniture exactly the way it was.
Some additional fees apply.

Q)  If I choose a traditional wood service, does it
cause a lot of dust?
A)  We respect your home the same as you. Before any
wood service, we do a thoroughly plastic cover off all
doors, rooms and areas that aren’t to be sanded. We
cover kitchen cabinets, appliances and fixtures to
minimize dust. However it is important to note that the
sanding dust is very fine and will filter through nearly
any barrier, our goal is to minimize it.

Timing of projects

Q)  How long does it take to install a new floor?
A)  Your Personal Design Consultant will determine the
timing of your project so you know what to expect.
While the length varies for each individual project,
typical timeframes are:

  • 1-2 days for carpet, vinyl and laminates
  • 2-3 days for tile and prefinished hardwoods
  • 4-8 days for traditional wood services

Q)  Why do traditional wood services take
longer?
A)  Your wood floor can last a lifetime only with the
proper care. We want to protect your investment, so
we take no short cuts when laying, sanding and
finishing your floor. With the “three-coat finish”
process, each coat needs to be fully cured for 24
hours before another coat is applied. Don’t be
misled by companies that offer a “three-coat finish”
in one day.

Walkthroughs and warranties

Q)  Will you provide me with a final inspection?
A)  Yes, before we consider a job complete, we do a
mandatory walkthrough with you to make sure
you’re satisfied with our work. However, with our
Traditional wood services, walkthroughs aren't
done on the final day of the job. If problems arise
after the final coat, we will be happy to return and
address the issues.

Q)  Does your work come with a warranty?
A)  Yes, we offer a warranty so you don’t have to worry.
If you find something after the walkthrough you’re
not happy with, just give us a call. All our work is
backed by a one-year craftsmanship warranty.

Licensing and insurance

Q)  Are you licensed?

You verify this going to the state board’s web site
[link to http://www.cslb.ca.gov/], clicking on “Check

Q)  Are you insured to do commercial projects?

our general liability insurance covers up to one
million dollars.
