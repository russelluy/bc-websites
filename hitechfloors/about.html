COMPANY OVERVIEW

Everything you want,
all in one place

We’re Hi Tech Hardwood Floors, a flooring business specializing in all type of hardwood flooring and have been serving the Bay Area since the early 1990's.

We believe in the fine art of living. That’s why we stay at the forefront of superior designs, fashionable styling and quality installations. With our comprehensive selection of products and services, we can cover your entire floor just about any way imaginable.

As a family-owned business, we keep it all in house. Owner Andy Tran actively supervises and participates in each project. You’ll never worry about subcontractors coming in off the street. Everyone on our staff is a full-time, trained employee under our management. The result is a more controlled process you can feel good about. It’s the peace of mind experience we strive to achieve.
