CPFM and CLS drew up a service level agreement that laid out the details of how they would share resources. They jointly created a job description for the new facilities manager by identifying the skills, knowledge, and experience that would successfully execute the requirements of the job from each department’s perspective. In addition, both CPFM and CLS participated in the recruitment effort and conducted joint interviews of potential candidates.

Once the person is hired, they will act as a liaison between CLS and CPFM, although primarily reporting to CPFM. What do you think would be the best way for that person to effectively represent and communicate the needs of both CLS and CPFM?
