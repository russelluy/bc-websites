Bay Area Child Care Agency has been serving the Bay Area since 1948 and has proudly become the premier hotel child care company in North America. American ChildCare Services philosophy is to provide safe, fun, and creative children’s programs and hotel babysitters for traveling families. We have a home office in San Francisco. If you are traveling to one or more of our home office destinations we would be happy to send a sitter to the hotel of your choice. Corporations, trade shows, and associations may utilize our service anywhere in North America.

We specialize in Five Star hotels and their clients by offering professional, dependable, and outstanding child care staff. ACS believes our staff is our best asset. Each staff member is thoroughly screened, CPR certified and field tested by ACS. We currently have over one hundred experienced child care providers on our staff. Our child care providers are professional, experienced, ethnically diverse, and speak fluent English.

We are licensed, bonded and fully insured. Our references include every major hotel in San Francisco. Bay Area Child Care Agency is a member in good standing with the convention/tourism/visitors bureau of San Francisco.

Our professional, friendly staff can be identified by their photo ID name tags.
