"With the Children"


When you say those words, it's the best vacation or business trip you can take. And when your family visits San Francisco, it's very reassuring to know that there is someone to care for your children during your daytime appointments and evening functions -- the professionals of Bay Area Child Care Agency. When you're out of town, you can count on five star hotel baby-sitting at all downtown hotels in San Francisco.

 

We're Head of the Class


Since 1948, Bay Area Child Care Agency is the premier hotel babysitting company in America. For parents, we're peace of mind -- we're licensed, bonded and insured. For children, we're like new-found friends.

 

It's Easy To "Play"


To arrange a comfortable and convenient stay for the family, Bay Area Child Care Agency works with hotel concierges or, if you prefer, directly with you. You simply reserve the days and times for child care. At your request, we will also accompany and supervise children six years or older to local museums and attractions. Ask about our group child care programs.
