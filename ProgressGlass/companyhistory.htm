About Us

Progress Glass Co., Inc. is a Northern California Glazing Contractor serving the Greater San Francisco Bay Area for over 60 years. Our corporate office is located in San Francisco (25 Patterson St). Our North Bay Office, which is located in Cotati (1015 East Cotati Ave.) also houses our fabrication facility. Having control of our fabrication allows us the luxury of on time deliveries from Ukiah to San Jose and all points between. Progress Glass Co., Inc. provides a variety of glass services to its clients, including 24hrs. emergency services. The company is committed to providing timely, cost effective services to those it serves. Progress Glass Co., Inc. is also committed to being a leader in the Glass Industry. Since Progress Glass Co., Inc. is a family owned business, we take great pride in approaching every project with a personal touch and a professional manner.
