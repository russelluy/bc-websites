About Us. CIMA’s Custom Cabinets was established by Fernando Guerra together with his brother Jesus Lopez in the summer of 2003. Fernando and his brother have a total combined experience of 18 years in the industry starting out as highly skilled craftsmen in one of the peninsula’s fine cabinet and furniture manufacturer. Immediately after founding the company the brothers set out to manufacture their own line of made to order custom cabinetry from a small shop in Union City which was moved to a 4,500 sq. ft. facility in 2004.

To date CIMA custom cabinets have completed numerous kitchen and bath remodeling projects and supplied a variety of custom cabinets in partnership with select designers, kitchen and bath dealers and contractors. CIMA’s commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made them a favorite of customers with discriminating taste and unique requirements.

CIMA’s fully-customized and handcrafted cabinets are available in entry-level, mid-level and the high end. CIMA also offers comprehensive and wide ranging design services and their in-house designer can help you conceptualize and visualize your dream kitchen and/or bathroom.

Our showroom features the different levels of custom cabinets that we offer including custom countertop fabrication and tile works by E&F Marble and Granite. In–showroom consultation is available upon request and is by appointment only.
