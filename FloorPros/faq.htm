Answers to common questions

Products and services

Q) What flooring products can I choose from?

A) We install carpet, ceramic tile, luxury vinyl, laminate and hardwood from top-quality brands like Mohawk, Karndean, Alloc, BR-111, Columbia Flooring and Mirage.


Preparing for installation

Q) Do I need to remove our furniture?

A) We offer full service, and will move all your furniture from your home to start our work. When the floor is complete, we'll return furniture exactly the way it was. Some additional fees may apply.

Q) If I choose a traditional wood service, does it cause a lot of dust?

A) We respect your home the same as you. Before any wood service, we do prepare zip walls & dust barriers to rooms and areas that aren't to be sanded. We cover kitchen cabinets, appliances and fixtures to minimize dust. However it is important to note that the sanding dust is very fine and will filter through nearly any barrier, our goal is to minimize it.


Q) Why do traditional wood services take longer?

A) Your wood floor can last a lifetime only with the proper care. We want to protect your investment, so we take no short cuts when laying, sanding and finishing your floor. With the "three-coat finish" process, each coat needs to be fully cured for 12 hours before another coat is applied. Don't be misled by companies that offer a "three-coat finish" in one day.
