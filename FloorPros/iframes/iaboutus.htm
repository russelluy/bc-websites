Since the early 1980s, Floor Pros Hardwood Floors has been adding style, beauty and value to Bay Area homes.

We are a family-owned and managed flooring contracting business specializing in residential and commercial Hardwood flooring installation. We offer a vast selection of new flooring products, including hardwoods, laminates , carpet, ceramic tile and luxury vinyl in a wide variety of styles, colors and textures. We also sand & refinish, repair, restore your existing hardwood floors.

Our mission is to help our clients explore the almost endless flooring possibilities available to them. We are familiar with the latest products, services and trends in our industry and are ready to help you bring your vision to life.
