Since 1980, Golden Crown Hardwood has been resorting, installing, sanding, staining and finishing floors in homes and businesses throughout the Bay Area.

Hardwood floors add beauty and value to any setting and have many advantages over other types of flooring. Hardwood is a classic look that will never go out of style and, when properly cared for, will last a lifetime. It's the easiest of all floor surfaces to keep clean and beautiful. If you have allergies, hardwood is a perfect choice - allergens can't hide in hardwood the way they can in carpeting. Hardwood is also renewable and recyclable product which does not create environmental waste.

 

Golden Crown employs a team of hardwood flooring specialists who work under strict quality guidelines. From start to finish, Golden Crown can assist you in every step of producing a beautiful hardwood floor. Call us for an absolutely free in-home estimate.
