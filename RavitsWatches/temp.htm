Welcome!

Ravits, established 24 years ago in Downtown San Francisco as a retail sales and service in watches. Family owned, our goal from the beginning has been to provide the finest quality and most comprehensive service possible. We repair all ranges of timepieces both modern and vintage, difficult repairs, and complications. We provide free estimates, fast turn around time, and are backed by a one-year warranty on all complete overhaul jobs. We do same-day watch battery replacement or while-u-wait in most cases. A huge inventory is available on watchband replacements to accommodate your needs. Special orders are never a problem, but a pleasure. Let us have a chance to be of service to you.
