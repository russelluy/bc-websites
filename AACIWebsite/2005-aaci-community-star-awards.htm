News: 2005 AACI Community Star Awards

This year, AACI presented the Community Star Awards to exceptional heroes who embodied leadership through community service and advocacy. The awardees have not only shown excellence in their respective fields, but generosity and character when giving back to the community.

We honor those leaders who have made outstanding contributions in at least one of the following areas: education, community and volunteerism, media and communications, youth leadership, business and technology arts, and/or advocacy

The Gordon N. Chan Leadership Award -

Bobbie J. Kawazoe

 

For over 26 years, Bobbie Kawazoe has dedicated herself to the community as an educator, counselor and public speaker. She has served as an AACI Board Member, Commissioner for the Status of Women, the Santa Clara County Transportation Committee, is currently on the Community Health Advisory Committee and is a lifetime member of the NAACP. Bobbie has received accolades for her hard work and dedication, such as the President’s Award from the National Association for the Advancement of Colored People, and a Commendation in the Field of Education from the Office of the Mayor of San Jose.
