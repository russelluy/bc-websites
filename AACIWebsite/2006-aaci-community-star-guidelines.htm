News: 2006 AACI Community Star Guidelines

Each year, Asian Americans for Community Involvement (AACI) presents AACI’s Community Star Awards to exceptional, often unsung heroes who embody leadership through community service and advocacy. AACI’s Community Star Awards grew out of our hope to recognize individuals from various sectors and aspects of the community. We want to acknowledge those who not only excel in their particular professional fields, but also possess strength of character and generosity of spirit, especially when it comes to giving back to the community.

We invite you to nominate an outstanding community leader or organization for an AACI Community Star Award. Please review the Nomination Guidelines below and fill out a nomination form. Any individual, corporation, group business, or community-based organization in the Bay Area can nominate an individual for AACI's Community Star Awards. Awardees will then be announced during AACI’s Annual Banquet on May 19, 2006.

NOMINATION GUIDELINES

AACI's Community Star Awards

Any individual or organization nominated must meet the following criteria:

  • Makes significant community service contributions, particularly in (but not limited to) the Asian Pacific American community
  • Has a history of helping the community beyond any one particular group/ethnicity
  • Has a high standard of ethics, character, and selflessness
  • Demonstrates excellence and leadership in their own area of expertise
  • Currently lives or is located in the California Bay Area

 

Any individual or organization in Silicon Valley who has recently made outstanding contributions in at least one of the following areas. Please note awards may not be given in all eight categories

  • Arts/Athletics
  • Communication & Media
  • Business/Corporate/Professions/Technology
  • Community/Volunteerism/Public Service
  • Education
  • Youth Leadership
  • Advocacy
  • Health and Human Services

 

All nominees should be available to attend the AACI 33rdAnnual Banquet on May 19, 2006 in order to beconsidered for the Award.
