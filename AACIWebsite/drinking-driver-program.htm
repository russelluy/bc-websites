Drinking Driver Program

Contact:
Nina Rivers, Program Manager
(408) 975-2730 x175
nina.rivers@aaci.org

AACI's Drinking Driver Program provides education and intervention to convicted First Offenders and Repeat Offenders on issues regarding alcohol and other substance abuse.   Services are provided in English & Spanish, as well as Vietnamese, Filipino, Chinese, Laotian, Korean, Khmer, Japanese, and Thai.

The program helps participants recognize their chemical dependency and supports them in recovery. Twin goals for participants include total abstinence from alcohol and illicit substance use, as well as meeting sobriety requirements.   95% of our clients graduate from the program; one of the best graduation rates in California.

Clients are primarily Santa Clara Court and DMV referred.   We serve English & Spanish speaking offenders, as well as Vietnamese, Filipino, Chinese, Laotian, Korean, Khmer, Japanese, and Thai offenders.
