Health Education and Training Center: Events


A regional conference for physicians, nurse practitioners, prevention specialists, and other health care providers organized by a partnership of the following agencies:

This year's HIV Care and Prevention conference will feature some of the leading experts in the field from throughout California presenting state of the art updates on the hottest topics in HIV care and prevention including drug treatment, mental health, prevention with specific communities, and the latest epidemiology. Exhibitor tables will also spotlight the work of local community agencies and resources. The sessions in this regional conference are primarily intended for physicians, nurse practitioners, prevention specialists, and other health professionals throughout the south San Francisco bay area.
