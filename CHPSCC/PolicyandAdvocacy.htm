Policy & Advocacy

Government Relations and Public Policy

  • Advocate for system changes to ensure that vulnerable populations are adequately served.
  • Monitor legislation and mange a coordinated response, including media advocacy.
  • Work with local and state policy makers on the needs of the nonprofit community health centers and the populations they serve.
  • Represent members in the California Primary Care Association.
  • Coordinate a grassroots collaborative policy and advocacy program network that focuses on healthcare issues related to community health centers, women's health, children's health, and immigrant issues.
