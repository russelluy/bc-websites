El Pueblo Agaisnt AIDS

El Pueblo Against AIDS , Health Education and Training Center 's HIV prevention and education program, provides HIV prevention and education to the public in general and to Latinos in particular. El Pueblo Against AIDS also serves as an advocate and resource for Latinos with HIV to gain access to HIV medical treatment, services and referral. Staff members are bilingual, bicultural and certified by the American Red Cross as HIV instructors, and are dedicated to providing HIV prevention and education information, resources and referral services to Latinos and the community.

El Pueblo Against AIDS developed "Fuera Mascaras" the first coming out brochure for Latinos. It has been hailed as the first of its kind by the Latino Gay and Lesbian Organization (LLEGO) and Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). This brochure has reached virtually every Spanish-speaking country in the world.

Current Program: Trans-Powerment
The Trans-Powerment Program is designed to address issues faced by transgender women who are HIV positive or negative. Our goal is to reduce HIV infection and transmission among transgenders with an emphasis on Latina and Asian trans-women and their partners.
