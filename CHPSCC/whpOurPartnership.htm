Women's Health Partnership: Our Partnership  

The Women's Health Partnership member base includes representatives from community-based organizations, women's health advocates, elected officials, health professionals, and other interested participants including members of the populations we serve. WHP holds meetings for all its members the first month of every quarter starting in July. The meetings are usually from 12:00 to 2:00 pm , and include program updates, presentations on topics of interest, opportunities to meet other like-minded individuals in the field of women's health, celebrate our accomplishments and strategize solutions to meet our challenges.

You can become a member on many levels. You may fill out our member profile form on this site and simply receive meeting announcements, newsletters, and other choice mailings. You may also choose a more active role by joining one of our sub-committees, volunteer at our events or share your expertise and resources on specific occasions. We would be happy to have your input wherever you feel comfortable. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact any of the WHP staff at (408) 289-9260.
