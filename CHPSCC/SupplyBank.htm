Community Diabetes Project: Supply Bank

In its 4th year of operation, the Diabetes Supply Bank seeks to offset the cost of accessing supplies for underserved Type 2 diabetics in the County while they are in the process of enrolling into long-term health care. The Bank offers an affordable temporary solution to diabetics who are uninsured or underinsured and unable to afford the high retail prices of diabetic supplies. For those who do not qualify for any private or public assistance, the Supply Bank offers a longer term affordable solution and those who are unemployed and homeless receive free supplies.
