Community Diabetes Project: Collaboration Development

Diabetes Coalition of Santa Clara County

The mission of the Diabetes Coalition of Santa Clara County is to improve the health and well being of low income, under-served people at risk for, or living with, diabetes through an integrated system of care.

The Diabetes Coalition of Santa Clara County has been in existence since March 2002. The Health Trust, Community Health Partnership, and the Diabetes Society of Santa Clara Valley, along with other provider groups, non-profit organizations, researchers, health plans and local hospitals, nurse parish programs, and local foundations established the Coalition to fulfill a need expressed by organizations working in the field of diabetes.
The Coalition identified four priorities as follows:

  • To develop a long-term strategy to standardize the system of care around diabetes in Santa Clara County.
  • To develop a prevention plan that targets children and adults in order to prevent the onset of diabetes; and to assess what prevention services are already available in the community.
  • To increase awareness of diabetes and work collaboratively around diabetes policy issues in Santa Clara County.
  • To support service gaps in the community, including funding and shortage of health professionals unavailable to provide services to people living with diabetes.
