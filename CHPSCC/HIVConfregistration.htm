HIV Care and Prevention 2006: Participant Registration



Click here to download and fax a registration form or register online. Deadline to submit online or fax registration in March 17, 2006.

To complete your registration, you will need to bring the following to the registration table at the conference:

  • A copy of your email confirmation. You will receive an email confirming that we received your registration information within 48 hours after completion.
  • Participant Information Form (PIF): Your PIF can be found by clicking the links above.
  • Proof of payment

Method of Payment
Registration fees are $50 per participant. Breakfast and lunch will be provided. Fees will be used to support HIV services in the south bay area through the Community Health Partnership and the Ira Greene Trust Fund.

Fees can be paid using Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, Paypal account, check, or purchase order. Checks and purchase orders should be made to “Community Health Partnership.” Registrations without full payment will not be accepted.

For additional information, contact Lydia Guel.

Cancellation and Refund Policy
Unfortunately, we are unable to refund any registration fees due to cancellation. Please refer all registration questions to Lydia Guel.
