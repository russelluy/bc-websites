Outside the Box

The purpose of Outside the Box is to share the success of Community Health Partnership and our member clinics. Each issue will focus on current policy and advocacy efforts, program updates, current collaborative developments, and fund development activities. Two special features will also be included: "Best Practices" for health care providers and an in-depth profile of a local community health clinic.
