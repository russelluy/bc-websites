Women's Health Partnership: Our Mission

Mid-life women in Santa Clara County face significant obstacles when seeking accessible, affordable health services. There is an increasing public focus to provide access to healthcare for all members of our community, but the growing population of low income mid-life women continues to be a public health concern that is overlooked.

There are upwards of 30,000 low income medically underserved women over the age of 40 living in Santa Clara County . Just when these women are approaching the age when many chronic illnesses manifest, they find themselves ineligible for public assistance programs such as Medi-Cal and MediCare. They may not qualify due to welfare reform because their children have entered adulthood, or because they are too young for MediCare.

The Women's Health Partnership was created to address the needs of this population.
