HIV Care and Prevention 2006: Exhibitor Opportunities



Hosting an exhibit booth will give your agency or company an opportunity to showcase HIV/AIDS-related information and resources for approximately 250 HIV care and prevention providers all over the south bay community.

The exhibit hall is strategically located in the Bayshore Foyer to allow maximum attendance of conference attendees. To view a layout of the conference, click “Venue and Travel Information” below.

Deadline to submit a request for an exhibitor table is March 10, 2006 . For more specific information regarding exhibitor opportunities or to request a table, please email SusanC@healthtrust.org .

Exhibitor Benefits

•  Exhibitor space will consist of a 6' draped rectangular table and two chairs. More than one table may be requested if necessary.

•  Exhibitors will be listed in the conference program.

•  Two conference registrations are included in the exhibitor package. No additional registrations will be provided.

•  The Double Tree Hotel provides general lighting, carpeting and power access upon request. Other needs and associated costs are the responsibility of the individual exhibitor.

Exhibitor Fees
Exhibit space is limited and may sell out. Priority will be given to community based organizations, as the planning committee is committed to providing a venue to spotlight the work of our local agencies. There is no fee for local community based organizations and agencies. Should space be available, businesses and companies seeking to exhibit HIV-related information may request a booth space for a fee of $800. The placement of your exhibit and the acceptance of your exhibit application are at the sole discretion of the HIV Care and Prevention 2006 planning committee.

Exhibitor Setup Access
Exhibitors will have access to the Bayshore Foyer beginning April 4, 2006 at 6:00 a.m. All exhibits must be installed and ready for the exhibit hall opening at 7:45 a.m. All exhibits must be dismantled immediately after the closing of the conference April 4, 2006 , starting at 5:00 p.m. and ending at 7:00 p.m. No early dismantling of exhibits is allowed .
