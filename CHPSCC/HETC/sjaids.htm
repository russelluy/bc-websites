San Jose Aids Education & Training Center (SJAETC)

Mission

•  To provide health care professionals with the knowledge and skills necessary to care for HIV-infected patients in underserved and vulnerable populations.
•  To increase the numbers of trained health care professionals working with HIV-infected patients.
•  To respond to the needs of high-risk populations and the changing face of the epidemic.

Services Offered

 Target Audience
We offer education and training programs specifically designed for:

•  Physicians
•  Nurses
•  Physician Assistants
•  Nurse Practitioners
•  Dentists
•  Dental Hygienists
•  Pharmacists
•  Other health care professionals

Clinical Training Programs
We offer clinical training programs for busy practitioners on the most current information on the management of HIV-infected patients and interventions to prevent high-risk behavior. Training is adapted to high, medium, or low volume providers.

Trainings include:
•  Lectures
•  Workshops, seminars and hands-on clinical experience
•  Intensive clinical rotations, preceptorships and mini-residencies
•  Clinical consultation on all aspects of managing patient care
•  Technical assistance in HIV quality of care

Our faculty are recognized clinical care experts and include physicians, nurse practitioners, nurses, pharmacists and dentists. Call us to find out about training opportunities. Continuing education credit is available for many programs.

Information Dissemination
Questions about care often arise due to the complexities of HIV infection. We provide resources to assist clinicians with difficult decisions. Resources include the latest treatment guidelines, slides, videos, and landmark journal articles. Teaching aides, such as training curricula and slide sets, on many topics are also available.
