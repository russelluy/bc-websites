Health Education and Training Center

MISSION

Health Education and Training Center (HETC) is a catalyst for change that improves and promotes personal, family and community health.

HISTORY

HETC was established in 1981 to respond to a noted lack of access to health services and education for low-income and ethnically-diverse communities in San Mateo and Santa Clara counties. HETC was initially established with Federal and California state funding to operate as an Area Health Education Center (AHEC), part of a national program to improve the accessibility and quality of health care for all citizens. Since, HETC has effectively addressed important community issues such as HIV prevention, tobacco prevention, youth development, cultural competency training for health professionals and emergency preparedness. HETC joined the Community Health Partnership in 2004.
