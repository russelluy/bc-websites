AHEC Healthy Futures

Mission
Children and youth in Santa Clara County will realize social, emotional, spiritual and physical health.  Our goal is to provide empowering opportunities for youth to develop into health literate individuals with a lifelong commitment to healthy living.  

Overview
Staff at HETC coordinate and conduct creative and innovative health education programs at Andrew Hill High School 's Medical Magnet School that build on the strengths and assets of youth. The Healthy Futures program provides scientifically and theoretically based information with approaches that develop positive health attitudes and behaviors and incorporate a wide range of learning styles, activities and teaching strategies. Programs are provided where youth learn, work and play, wherever the appropriate venue is determined by participants.

Youth are engaged on a variety of levels to develop knowledge, skills, attitudes, and behaviors that will make health education not the presentation of a set of facts to be studied, memorized, and quickly forgotten but a meaningful part of their lives. Student input and participation in program design and direction is essential and encouraged. Our focus is on students realizing their control over their health, incorporating health related knowledge into everyday behavior, and making a lifelong commitment to healthy living.
