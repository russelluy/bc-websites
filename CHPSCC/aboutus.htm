Even in a community
dedicated to progress, more than
250,000 Silicon Valley residents
remain uninsured.

Too many families find themselves without access to healthcare because they are ineligible for employee-based health insurance of Medi-Cal, are unable to pay for costly health insurance premiums, or are fearful of government programs.

The number of uninsured patients served by nonprofit community health centers continues to rise every year. In today's healthcare environment, these health centers are challenged to maintain their survival.

Community Health Partnership is a vital community asset because it offers important services to these community health centers. With services designed to create opportunities for collaboration and to present a strong, unified voice, Community Health Partnership enables these health centers to maximize resources. The result is a stronger healthcare safety net and improved healthcare access for underserved, vulnerable populations.

Mission
The mission of the Partnership is to expand access to health services for the medically underserved, encourage prevention and health promotion through service, education and advocacy. The Partnership is committed to developing affordable health services that meet the needs of our diverse multi-cultural community.

Vision
Community Health Partnership will enhance its position as a viable healthcare safety-net in the Santa Clara County area and serve as a model among the nation's most respected public-private healthcare partnerships. In fulfilling this role, Community Health Partnership will:

  • Foster a culture that provides responsive and compassionate care.
  • Provide distinguished programs and services of excellence that build upon a tradition of quality clinical practice, medical education, and community service.
  • Provide superior capacities, facilities, and technology for the full continuum of healthcare services.
  • Ensure that all persons achieve the most optimal health possible regardless of their socioeconomic, ethnic, religious, or cultural background.
