Women's Health Partnership: Gabriella Patser Program

The Gabriella Patser Program of the Community Breast Health Project (CBHP) provides diagnostic services for low income, uninsured women under the age of 40 who have a breast health problem that is suspicious of breast cancer. The program has served the community since 1995 and also offers navigational, educational, and emotional support. All services are free of charge. The Gabriella Patser Program is funded by individual donors and foundation support.

Eligibility :

•  Be under 40 years of age,
•  Have a breast health problem suspicious of a malignancy or have a strong family history of breast cancer ( 2 first degree relatives),
•  Live in Santa Clara or San Mateo County or receive primary care medical services in these counties,
•  Be uninsured, and
•  Have a family income less than or equal to 200% of the Federal Poverty Level.

For more information, please call 650-326-6686 or visit www.cbhp.org.
