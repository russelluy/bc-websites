Garage Door Maintenance:

 

To keep your garage door working smoothly, set some time aside once a year to perform maintenance.

 

- Clean the oil and grease build up from the tracks. Dirt combined with the grease and oil can cost the door to bind and jump.

- Re-lubricate the tracks with a quality grease and the rollers with a quality spray.

- Oil all of the hinge and retighten all knots and bolts.

- Check for wear, including wear along the rails, smoothness of operation, and cracks in the springs.

 

To protect your garage door from moisture related deterioration:

 

- Paint all exposed wood.

- Make sure weather stripping is in good condition.

- Keep water from collecting from the base of the door.

-- Clear nearby drains and gutters of debris, and correct any existing drainage problems.
