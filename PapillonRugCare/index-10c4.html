Enjilas

Enjilas is situated to the south of Hamadan at the foot of Mount Alvand. There are two types of designs attributed to the town of Enjilas. The first is Boteh-miri and the second is Herati. They are both reproduced in this example.

Esfahan

This rug is design with a garden in full bloom with graceful arabesques. The wide main border is decorated in red with bouquets of flowers.
