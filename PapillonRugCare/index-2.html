about us:

 

Since 1994, California's San Francisco Bay Area - Based Rug Cleaning and Sales establishment, Papillon Rug Care, has been devoted to working with Persian Rugs 

and Oriental Carpets enthusiasts from all over the world.

 

From a family home to an engaging show room, Papillon Rug Care can help you decorate with distinctive textures and colors, or simply help preserve the quality of carpeting you already own.

 

Papillon Rug Care is operated by Auserehlian Certified Technicians.Clients will 

discover what expert service and experience is all about. Pls. Visit or call our office

to learn first hand what Papillon Rug Care has to offer.

 

With the friends PRC has made over the last 10yrs., the owner has access to a network of professionals who specialize in one-of-a-kind Rugs and Carpets featuring original designs and gorgeous color combinations.

 

Please contact us for any questions, we are looking forward to meeting your needs and furthering your expectations!
