Baluchi

These rugs are made in the Mashad region near the town of Torbat-e-Heidarieh. This design has few colors and its pattern mainly consists of stylized flowers.

Bakhtiari

The Turkish knot used to make this rug can be attributed to the large number of Turkish immigrants to the Chahar Mahal region. The village of Shalamzar, where this rug was made has a high reputation of quality craftsmanship.
